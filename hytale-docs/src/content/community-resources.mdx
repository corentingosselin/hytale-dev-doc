- **URL**: [code.visualstudio.com](https://code.visualstudio.com)

## Plugin Distribution Platforms

### Community Discussion: Mod Distribution & Tooling
The community expects mod/plugin distribution to remain community-run (similar to Minecraft) rather than an official marketplace. These expectations are not official policy and may change when Hypixel Studios publishes formal guidance.

### CurseForge
- **URL**: [curseforge.com](https://www.curseforge.com/hytale)
- **Status**: Already has Hytale section
- **Community**: Established platform from Minecraft

### Modrinth
- **URL**: [modrinth.com](https://modrinth.com)
- **Status**: Community wants Hytale support (currently Minecraft-focused)
- **Features**: Modern, fast, open-source friendly

### BuiltByBit (BBB)
- **URL**: [builtbybit.com](https://builtbybit.com)
- **Status**: Community marketplace for plugins/resources
- **Notes**: Often abbreviated as BBB in discussions

### GitHub Releases
- **Direct distribution** from your repository
- **Control**: Full control over releases
- **Community**: Standard practice for open-source plugins
